Crystals Collector

Below are four crystals. They are randomly assigned a number between 1-9.
The aim of the game is to reach the target value.
Click on the crystals to reach the target value. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will change and the random number will change.
